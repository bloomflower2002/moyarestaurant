About Moya Café & Kitchen

Welcome to Moya Café & Kitchen, a warm and inviting space where rustic charm meets culinary artistry. Nestled in the heart of the Shenandoah Valley, just a short drive from the city, Moya offers an unforgettable farm-to-table dining experience. Our team is passionate about crafting fresh, seasonal dishes inspired by local ingredients and global flavors. Whether you're here for brunch, a relaxing afternoon coffee, or a romantic dinner, Moya Café & Kitchen is your perfect countryside escape.

Indulge in an authentic Ethiopian dining experience without leaving the city. We bring you the soulful flavors of Habesha cuisine, made with the freshest ingredients, for a true taste of home.
